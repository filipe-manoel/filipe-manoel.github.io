About me

I am a Forest Engineer and master in Genetics and Breeding for the University of Viçosa (UFV) in Brazil. Nowdays, I am a Ph.D. student in Genetics and Breeding at UFV. My research interests include quantitative genetics, genomic selection and applied statistics for plant breeding. Currently, I am focused on perennial species breeding, such as forages and trees Download my CV
