About me

I’m an Forest Engineer and master in Genetics and Breeding for the University of Viçosa (UFV) in Brazil. I’m a Ph.D. student in Genetics and Breeding at UFV. My research interests include quantitative genetics, genomic selection and applied statistics for plant breeding. Currently, I’m focused on perennial species breeding, such as forages and trees. Download my CV
